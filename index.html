Shruthi Chari

I am a Computer Science PhD candidate at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute advised by Professor Deborah L. McGuinness and Professor Oshani Seneviratne. My research interests lie in the area of Clinical Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI). Specifically, I apply semantics, machine learning, and natural language processing techniques to further the fields of human-centered explainable AI and health informatics..

Previously, I received my B.S. in Computer Science at PES Institute of Technology and M.S. in Computer Science at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.
